Welcome to SOL Chamber Orchestra

We are a self-led chamber orchestra of unique individuals forging connections in our community through chamber music and orchestral concerts.

we build bridges between performers and audience, between classical and popular genres, and between our Community and the issues of our times.

We demonstrate the power of respectful dialogue, democratic leadership, and caring connections to Serve our community, and we work to transmit these values to the next generation.
